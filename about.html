About this website and me

Why did I create this website?

I created this website for a number of reasons. During the COVID-19 pandemic I bought a new PC, I originally bought this for a bit of gaming but along the way I thought that I would learn how to code. I am currently studying a full stack developer course with The Oden Project. TOP is pretty cool and free, I talk more about them in the coding section of this site.

Early on in in my studying I learnt the basics of HTML, CSS and JavaScript and then moved on to Ruby. When I was starting to learn Ruby I realised that my brain is like a sieve and I have a terrible memory, I decided to create this website alongside my studies to apply and develop what I have learnt and reduce any skill fade.

The first problem I came across when building this site was, what would it be about? I thought that as I am currently learning to code I will do it about this and the experience I have had while also giving me a platform to demonstrate what I have learnt. I also like keeping fit so I thought I would chuck a bit of that in here and anything else random I have done along the way.
